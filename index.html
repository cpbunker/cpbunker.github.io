About Me

I am a graduate student in the physics department of the University of Florida. I am interested in computational physics and quantum mechanics, with a focus on how these approaches can be used to investigate exciting systems in condensed matter physics and nanomaterials. I enjoy studying and constructing physical models of these systems and determining how thet can achieve maximum predictive and explanatory power.
