About Me

I am a graduate student in the physics department of the University of Florida. I am interested in computational physics and statistical mechanics, with a focus on how these approaches can be used to investigate complex quantum mechanical and biophysical systems. I enjoy studying physical models and how implementation of simple rules can explain emergent behaviors.
